## HR Analysis Dashboard

### Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing HR data to understand workforce composition, employee satisfaction, recruitment sources, and employee status. The dashboard helps identify important patterns related to hiring channels, departmental distribution, demographics, and employee retention.

### Key Insights

- Nearly **50% of employees were hired through Indeed and LinkedIn**, making them the most effective recruitment channels for the organization.
- The **average employee satisfaction score ranges from moderate to high**, indicating a generally positive workplace environment.
- The company has a **higher proportion of female employees (56%) compared to male employees (44%)**, showing a relatively balanced gender distribution.
- The **Production department employs around 70% of the total workforce**, making it the largest department in the company.
- Approximately **44% of employees are single**, with the majority belonging to the **under 30 age group**.
- Out of **311 total employees, around 220 employees are currently active**, while **about 25% of employees have voluntarily left the organization**.

### Conclusion
The analysis highlights key workforce trends, including the dominance of online recruitment platforms, a strong presence of employees in the production department, and moderate to high employee satisfaction levels. These insights can help HR teams improve hiring strategies, workforce planning, and employee retention initiatives.
